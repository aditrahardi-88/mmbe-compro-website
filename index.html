About Us

PT Mega Makmur Berkat Energi is a company engaged in large-scale trade focused on coal trading and coal transport services for local and foreign markets. We emphasize customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility through our Total Quality & Environmental Management approach. We build our business by focusing on service quality, efficient operations and market-driven insights.

Quick facts

  • Focus: Coal trade & transport (local + international)
  • Delivery capability: full transshipment projects within 27–30 days
  • Reporting: Pre-shipment analysis, lab results, daily loading reports.

Our Services

  • Coal trading for local and international markets
  • Coal transport & transshipment (barge & mother vessel coordination)
  • Pre-shipment inspection & lab quality reports
  • Daily loading reports and operational monitoring

We provide detailed activity reports covering location, physical quality, lab results, loading rates and daily updates.

Operational Framework

Day 1 — Buyer LOI → MMBE Full Corporate Offer (FCO)
Day 2–10 — SPA draft & LC verbiage, legal & commercial preparation
Day 5–10 — SPA + FCO approval, MMBE-Funder agreement
Day 8–12 — Coal SPA signing, LC acceptance & activation
Day 12–20 — Logistics: barge rental by MMBE-funder, MV rental by buyer
Day 16–27 — Barge loading, transshipment, MV loading, documentation
End — LC monetization & final delivery

Estimated completion for a large-scale transshipment project: 27–30 days.
